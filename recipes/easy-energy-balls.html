Easy Energy Balls

Description

My friend showed me this recipe and I'm obsessed with them. These are quick
easy to make, and they are delicious! Serve chilled, if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup instant oats
  • ⅔ cup flaked coconut
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • ½ cup flax seeds
  • ½ cup chocolate chips
  • ¼ cup honey

Steps

  1. Stir oats, coconut, peanut butter, flax seeds, chocolate chips, and honey together in a large bowl
    until well-combined. Roll into golf ball-size balls.
